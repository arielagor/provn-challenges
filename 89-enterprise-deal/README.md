**Role archetype:** Enterprise AE

## Brief (paraphrased)

A strategic enterprise deal at Meridian (fictional target) has stalled at the CFO. The champion is Dana (procurement VP), the economic buyer is Marcus (CFO) under pressure for a 12% discretionary spend cut, and a competitor (Coupa) is positioning for a "strategic partnership" meeting. You have 27 business days to a signed contract before fiscal close, or it slips a year. Produce an executive brief for the CFO and a full deal assessment with a close plan, competitive strategy, objection handling, and post-close expansion.
