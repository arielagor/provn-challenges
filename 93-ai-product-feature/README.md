# Challenge 93 — Define and De-Risk an AI Feature With 6 Weeks, 3 Engineers, and No ML Team

**Role archetype:** AI Product Manager  
**PROVN page:** https://provn.co/challenge-details/93  
**Submitted:** 2026-04-23

## Brief (paraphrased)

You're the AI PM at a mid-size SaaS. Leadership wants an AI feature shipped in 6 weeks with 3 engineers and no ML team. Pick the feature, de-risk it, set success metrics, and produce a build plan the engineers can actually execute. Scope honestly — say no to the ideas that don't fit the constraints.
